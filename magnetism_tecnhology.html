Technologies Based on Magnetism

Introduction

Magnetism is the foundation of numerous technologies that enable the operation of electrical systems, transportation, medicine, digital storage, and communications. Its application is essential in modern society.

Technological Applications

Motors and Generators

They operate through the interaction between magnetic fields and electric currents. Motors convert electricity into movement, while generators do the opposite.

Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)

It uses strong magnetic fields and radio waves to generate internal images of the human body. It is indispensable for diagnostic medicine.

Magnetic Levitation Trains (Maglev)

They employ superconducting electromagnets to levitate and propel trains at speeds above 500 km/h without mechanical friction.

Magnetic Storage

Hard drives, magnetic tapes, and cards use magnetic layers to store information in the form of oriented domains.

Industrial Electromagnets

Used to lift heavy metals, separate materials, and operate machinery in industrial environments.
